How to Play

It's you against Pixel Dealer. Your goal is to get as close to 21 as possible without going over.

Both you and the Dealer start with two cards. The Dealer shows one card face up, and keeps the other face down.

  • Cards 2-10 are worth their number
  • Face (J, Q, K) cards are worth 10
  • Aces are flexible and can count as 1 or 11

You can play your turn by choosing one of these options:

  • HIT - Draw another card.
  • STAND - Hold your hand and end your turn.
  • SPLIT - If you're dealt two cards of the same rank, you can split them into two hands.
  • DOUBLE DOWN - Double your bet for one more card only.

After your turn, the dealer then reveals their face-down card and plays, hitting until they reach at least 17.

If you go over 21, it's a BUST. If you tie with the dealer, it's a PUSH. The closest to 21 wins!
