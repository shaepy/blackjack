How to Play

It's you versus the Dealer. Your goal is to get as close to 21 as you can without going over.

Both of you start with two cards. The dealer will show one card face up, and one face down.

  • Cards 2-10 are worth their number
  • Face (J, Q, K) cards are worth 10
  • Aces are flexible and can count as 11 or 1

You can HIT to draw another card, or STAND to hold your hand and end your turn.

The dealer then flips their hidden card and plays. They have to keep hitting until they reach at least 17.

Getting over 21 is a BUST. A tie is known as a PUSH. Whoever is closer to 21 wins!
